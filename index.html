About Me

Thanks for visiting my page! I am a senior Computer Science major at Colorado State University. My completed coursework includes team-based, long-term projects in an Agile/Scrum development environment.

During two software engineering internships with Lockheed Martin, I contributed to team-based software development, cross domain security solutions, and secure website design using the Django web framework. I delivered solutions that optimize project management for systems utilized on over 1000 installations worldwide, and developed web content accessible by over 12,000 Lockheed Martin employees.

Prior to pursuing a degree, I served in the United States Air Force, where I was an aircrew member and international liaison stationed overseas for five years. In this capacity, I held multiple supervisory roles and worked on numerous independent teams tasked with the safe operation of multimillion-dollar military assets. My experience as a military aviator taught me the value of strong communication, respect for cultural differences, and how to maximize these diverse views to achieve the best possible results.
